1. Who We Are

Cloud's Vista ("we", "us", "our") is a restaurant business located at DD'S Centrium, DBS Road, Central Area, Asaba 320108, Delta State, Nigeria. We operate the website thecloudsvista.com and are the data controller for information collected through our website, reservation system, and online ordering platform.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at info@thecloudsvista.com or call 0805 149 8689.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and password (stored as an encrypted hash) when you create an account.
  • Reservation details: Date, time, number of guests, occasion type, and special requests when you make a table booking.
  • Order information: Items ordered, quantities, total amount, and chosen payment method.
  • Contact form submissions: Name, email, phone, inquiry type, and message content.
  • Newsletter subscription: Email address if you opt in to our newsletter.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent — collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Payment data: We do not store card numbers or CVV codes. Payment processing is handled by trusted third-party platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm reservations and orders
  • Communicate with you via WhatsApp, phone, or email about your bookings and orders
  • Send you confirmation messages and receipts
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Send promotional emails and exclusive offers (only if you subscribed — you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve our website, menu, and services based on aggregate usage patterns
  • Comply with legal obligations

We only process your data where we have a lawful basis to do so — including your consent, contractual necessity (fulfilling your order), and our legitimate interests (improving our service).

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may share your data with the following categories of recipients only where necessary:

  • Delivery partners: Name and address for order fulfilment, shared only when a delivery order is placed.
  • Payment processors: Paystack, Flutterwave, or bank partners — only the minimum information required to process your payment.
  • WhatsApp Business: When you proceed to checkout, order details are shared with WhatsApp to initiate communication with our team. WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy applies to this interaction.
  • Analytics providers: Aggregated, anonymised usage data may be shared with analytics tools to help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. Account information is kept in encrypted local storage on your device and on our secured servers. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, or unauthorised disclosure.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, typically:

  • Account data: Until you request deletion
  • Order and reservation records: 3 years for accounting and service improvement purposes
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe
  • Technical logs: 90 days

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies on our website. For full details on the types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how to manage your preferences, please see our Cookie Policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on our legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@thecloudsvista.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites including Instagram, Facebook, Google Maps, and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify you via email (if you have an account with us) or through a notice on our website.

Your continued use of our website after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
