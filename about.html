Our Story

Who We Are

Born from a Vision

Cloud's Vista was founded with one clear mission: to create a dining experience in Asaba that doesn't just satisfy hunger, but nourishes the soul. Nestled in DD'S Centrium on DBS Road, our restaurant has become the go-to destination for those who believe that a great meal is about far more than the food on the plate.

From our very first day, we have poured our hearts into every detail — the ambient lighting that casts a warm golden glow, the rooftop terrace with its breathtaking top view, the thoughtfully curated menu that blends Nigerian culinary heritage with global influences.

When guests tell us they felt "at home" — especially those visiting Nigeria for the first time from thousands of miles away — we know we are living our mission.

The Atmosphere

A Space That Inspires

"Aesthetically it has its game on lock. The top view, the lighting, the choice in colours." — those words from a guest capture everything we have worked to build.

Our design philosophy is intentional: every corner of Cloud's Vista is a considered decision. The lighting is warm, almost amber — it makes every person in the room look their best and feel at ease. The terrace offers a top view of Asaba that few places can match.

We believe that ambiance is as important as the food itself. When you walk through our doors, you should feel the world outside slow down a little. Whether you're on a first date, celebrating a milestone, or simply unwinding after a long week, Cloud's Vista is designed to make that moment feel elevated.

What Drives Us

Our Values

Every decision we make — from how we source ingredients to how we greet you at the door — is guided by these principles.

Genuine Hospitality

We treat every guest like family. From your first reservation to your last bite, your comfort and joy are our priority — no exceptions.

Uncompromising Quality

Fresh ingredients, carefully prepared. Every dish that leaves our kitchen represents our commitment to excellence — we never cut corners.

Aesthetic Intentionality

Beauty is not accidental here. Every design choice — the lighting, the colours, the layout — is deliberate and serves a purpose.

Community Roots

We are proud to call Asaba home. We source locally where possible and strive to give back to the community that has embraced us.

Creative Spirit

From Eve's Apple Drink to our seasonal specials, we are always experimenting, always innovating — because good food should surprise you.

Trust & Transparency

We listen to feedback, own our mistakes and continuously improve. Your honest opinion helps us become better every single day.

Food & Drink

Crafted with Purpose

Our menu is a love letter to bold flavours — Nigerian classics reimagined with contemporary technique, paired with creative cocktails that have become part of Asaba's conversation.

The Eve's Apple Drink isn't just a cocktail; it's a signature. The Oreo City Smoothie isn't just a drink; it's an experience. The Peppered Gizzard isn't just a starter; it's a statement about who we are — rooted, flavourful, and unapologetically Nigerian.

We believe in freshness above all. Our bread is baked daily. Our cocktails are mixed to order. Our kitchen operates with the discipline of a fine dining establishment and the soul of a family-run restaurant.
