1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies can be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them). They can be set by the website you're visiting (first-party cookies) or by third parties whose services are embedded in the website.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preference so we don't ask you every time
  • Keep your shopping cart intact as you browse different pages
  • Keep you signed in to your account during your visit
  • Remember your newsletter subscription preference
  • Understand which pages and features are most popular
  • Improve the performance and functionality of our website
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third parties. These include:

  • Google Fonts & Google Maps: May set cookies related to font delivery and map embedding. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome (Cloudflare CDN): Icon fonts are loaded from Cloudflare's CDN. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp: When you initiate a WhatsApp conversation through our checkout, you are redirected to WhatsApp's platform. See WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash (image CDN): Product and background images may be loaded from Unsplash servers. See Unsplash's Privacy Policy.

We do not control third-party cookies and are not responsible for their policies. We recommend reviewing the relevant policies linked above.

6. Local Storage & Session Storage

In addition to cookies, we use HTML5 localStorage and sessionStorage to store data on your device. These technologies work similarly to cookies but store data locally in your browser without sending it to our servers with every request.

  • localStorage — persists until cleared: stores your account session (cv_user), registered users (cv_users), and cookie consent preference (cv_cookie_consent).
  • sessionStorage — cleared when you close the browser tab: stores your current cart contents (cv_cart).

You can clear all local storage data by going to your browser's developer tools (F12 → Application → Local Storage / Session Storage) and deleting the relevant keys, or by clearing your browser's site data.
